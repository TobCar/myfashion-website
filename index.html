About the Developer

Tobias Carryer

It all began when he was 10 years old learning how to make video games at a summer camp. Game design was fun, but after a few years he decided he should do something more serious. Programming after school and on weekends he learned to make mobile apps for Android and iOS. Now, he is releasing MyFashion into the world, one year after coming up with the idea. It is his first project to ever reach the public.
